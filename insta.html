Project Overview

Challenge:

A survey was conducted by our team through which we identified seven key areas where users expressed their desire for additional or altered features on Instagram, which we can also refer to as their pain points. Prioritizing these needs posed a challenge for the team until we collectively brainstormed and came up with a solution to all user pain points.

Our Solution:

Instagram Lux is a user-driven upgrade to Instagram. After analyzing data from 240 app users, we developed this upgrade to effectively address their pain points and meet their needs.

Target Audience:

All types of Instagram users.

Understanding the User

User Research:

We conducted user research through live interviews and surveys to gather data, which we subsequently utilized to create an affinity diagram. This allowed us to identify the most significant pain points experienced by current Instagram users. Additionally, we developed user personas to enhance our understanding and empathy towards Instagram users, enabling us to generate targeted solutions for their specific pain points.

Persona

User Pain Points

1. Fewer and/or no ads.

2. More control over content (algorithm) of what is seen.

3. Faster upload speeds.

4. Options for Instagram Reels (to remove if desired).

5. Removal of bots.

6. Improved image quality.

7. All types of Instagram users.

Starting the Design

Ideation:

To initiate the ideation process, I began by creating paper wireframes to strategically map out the placement of the Instagram Lux upgrade within the existing Instagram interface. The objective was to enable users to easily discover the upgrade and, consequently, persuade them to consider purchasing a subscription.

The paper wire-frames were transferred into Figma to create the initial digital wire-frames. These wire-frames served as the foundation for our low-fidelity prototypes, which I will later show your how they were used for user testing and iteration.

Study Findings

Back Button:

Users recommended the inclusion of a back or close button for every pop-up within the app.

Logo Iconography:

Create a smaller version of the Instagram Lux logo to strategically position on the screen to subtly encourage users to consider subscribing.

Refining the Design

Once the low-fidelity prototypes were tested and validated, we took the insights gained from user feedback and made sure to address the suggestions that were received in the creation of high-fidelity mockups. These mockups were designed to provide a more polished and visually appealing representation of the user interface, showcasing the final look and feel of the product.
